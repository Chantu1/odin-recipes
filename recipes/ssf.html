Ingredients

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sriracha sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger root
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 (12 ounce) package all-beef franks, cut into 1-inch slices
  • 1 box toothpicks

Steps

  1. Mix ketchup, soy sauce, vinegar, sriracha, brown sugar, sesame oil, ginger, and garlic in a saucepan
  2. Bring to a simmer over low heat
  3. Add franks and heat until warm for 5 minutes
  4. Serve with toothpicks
